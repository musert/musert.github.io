Research Interests

Multimodal Learning

Machine learning methods that jointly model information across text, audio, image, and video.

Audio, Vision and Language

Representation learning and semantic understanding for audio, visual and language-based AI tasks.

Semantic Multimedia

Machine learning methods for extracting semantic meaning from multimedia content.

Applied Intelligent Systems
